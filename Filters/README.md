# Kalmann Filters

Kalman filters are a cornerstone of modern engineering, enabling accurate estimation in the presence of uncertainty. From tracking a dog with RFID to guiding spacecraft to Mars, they provide a powerful and elegant framework for combining models with measurements. Understanding the different types and their applications helps you choose the right tool for solving our specific problem.
